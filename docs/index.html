ALIGN Global Hub

The ALIGN Global Hub is a market intelligence platform designed to support prioritization and introduction decisions for health products.

It integrates fragmented data across clinical development, regulatory systems, procurement platforms, and policy frameworks into a single, structured view—enabling comparison, forecasting, and decision-making.

Why this exists

Health product data are distributed across multiple systems, limiting the ability to:

  • Compare products across pipelines and applications
  • Anticipate what is coming to market
  • Align financing, procurement, and delivery planning
  • Support structured prioritization decisions

The Global Hub addresses this by integrating and standardizing data into a unified analytical framework.

What this platform supports

The Hub is designed to inform key decision points:

  • Prioritization — which products to consider
  • Sequencing — when to introduce them
  • Financing alignment — how timelines match budgets
  • System readiness — whether delivery systems are prepared

Scope

Current coverage focuses on high-priority applications:

  • HIV/AIDS
  • Tuberculosis
  • Malaria
  • Maternal, newborn, and child health (MNCH)

Products are categorized by type (therapeutics, vaccines, diagnostics, devices) and indication.

About ALIGN

The ALIGN Consortium supports governments in strengthening health product prioritization and introduction through:

  • Data-driven decision-making
  • Portfolio-based planning
  • Whole-of-government coordination
